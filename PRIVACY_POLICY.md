# Privacy Policy for Turtle

**Effective Date:** December 27, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 27, 2025

## Introduction

Turtle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Turtle mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

When you use Turtle, we automatically collect certain information through Firebase services:

- **Anonymous User ID**: A unique identifier assigned to your device to maintain game sessions
- **Game Session Data**: Temporary game state information including:
  - Player names (chosen by you during gameplay)
  - Answers submitted during games
  - Votes cast during games
  - Game scores and results
  - Game room codes (4-character codes for multiplayer sessions)

### Information You Provide

- **Player Name**: You choose a display name when joining or hosting a game
- **Submitted Answers**: Text answers you create during gameplay
- **Game Preferences**: Settings like chosen player themes (emoji avatars)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

- **Facilitate Multiplayer Gameplay**: Enable real-time game sessions between players
- **Synchronize Game State**: Ensure all players see the same questions, answers, and scores
- **Maintain Game Sessions**: Temporarily store game data during active play sessions
- **Provide Core Functionality**: Enable the App's trivia game features

## Data Storage and Retention

- **Temporary Storage**: All game session data is stored temporarily in Firebase Realtime Database during active gameplay
- **Automatic Deletion**: Game data is automatically deleted when:
  - The game session ends
  - Players leave the game
  - After 24 hours of inactivity
- **No Long-Term Storage**: We do not store your personal information or game history permanently
- **Local Storage**: Questions used in the game are stored locally on your device using SwiftData

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service:

### Firebase (Google LLC)

- **Purpose**: Real-time database for multiplayer synchronization and anonymous authentication
- **Data Collected**: Anonymous user IDs, game session data, real-time gameplay information
- **Firebase Privacy Policy**: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
- **Google Privacy Policy**: [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

Firebase may collect additional technical information such as:
- Device information (model, operating system version)
- IP addresses (for connection purposes)
- Usage analytics (if enabled)

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

- **Anonymous Authentication**: No email, password, or personal identification required
- **Encrypted Transmission**: All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
- **Firebase Security Rules**: Database access is controlled through Firebase security rules
- **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

## Children's Privacy

Turtle is designed as a family-friendly trivia game. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

- **No Personal Information Required**: The App does not require or collect personal information from users
- **Anonymous Play**: Players only provide temporary display names during gameplay
- **Parental Guidance**: We recommend parental supervision for children under 13

## Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request information about data collected during your game sessions
- **Deletion**: Stop using the App at any time; all temporary session data is automatically deleted
- **Opt-Out**: Choose not to use online multiplayer features by playing in local mode

## User-Generated Content

Players create answers during gameplay that are temporarily visible to other players in the same game session. By using the App, you agree that:

- Your submitted answers are shared with other players in your game session
- Answers should be appropriate and respectful
- We are not responsible for content created by other players
- Game sessions are temporary and not permanently recorded

## International Data Transfers

Firebase services may process data on servers located in various countries, including the United States. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your country of residence.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective when posted within the App or on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know whether personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
- Right to request deletion of personal information

**Note**: Turtle does not sell personal information and only collects minimal anonymous data for gameplay functionality.
